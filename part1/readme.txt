Part1
=====
 
 * It consist of experimental setup and results for Sections III, IV and V of our paper
   "Dataset artefacts in anti-spoofing systems: a case study on the ASVspoof 2017 benchmark"
   submitted to IEEE/ACM TASLP


This directory comprises two sub directories
  - original
  - interventions

original:       consists of experimental setup and results for five different models trained/validated and tested on the 
                original dataset containing artefacts.

interventions:  consists of experimental setup and results for inference time interventions on five different countermeasure models.
